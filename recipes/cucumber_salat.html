Cucumber Salat

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers, sliced into rounds
  • Garlic powder
  • Dried dill
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Bacon bits
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar chesse

Steps

  1. Place sliced cucumbers, garlic powder, dill, salt and pepper in a bowl. Toss to coat.
  2. Add green onions, bacon bits and ranch dressing; toss to coat.
  3. Add shredded chesse and toss one last time. Serve immediately.
