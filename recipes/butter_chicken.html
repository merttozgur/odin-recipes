Butter Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed buttery round cracker crumbs
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Garlic salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup butter, cut into pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients, Preheat the oven to 190°C.
  2. Place cracker crumbs and eggs into 2 separat shallow bowls. Mix cracker crumbs with garlic salt and pepper.
  3. Dip chicken in egg, then dredge in crumb mixture to coat.
  4. Arrange coated chicken in baking dish. Scatter butter over and around the chicken.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 40 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.
